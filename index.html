Quarantine Packages - Saudi Arabia

Passengers permitted to travel from India to Saudi Arabia can book quarantine packages through Air India Express. Bookings should be made 48 hours prior departure. Hotel Rooms will be on full board basis for minimum of 6 nights/7 days. Package also includes one way airport to hotel transfer and the required RT-PCR tests at the hotel.
